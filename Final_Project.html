My Childhood

One of my fondest childhood memories is the jingle of the ice cream truck on hot summer afternoons.
The excitement would send me sprinting to the street with loose change clutched in my hand. Choosing from a rainbow of frozen treats,
I'd eagerly savor the sweet joy of a dripping ice cream cone under the warm sun, creating a simple memory that still brings a smile to my face.
